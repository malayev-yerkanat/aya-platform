## Design Context

### Users
Kazakh independent creators and audiences — filmmakers, musicians, podcasters, documentarians — who want a platform that feels native, serious, and culturally relevant. Viewers are 18–35, culturally proud, tech-comfortable. Context: laptop/desktop evening viewing, similar to how Spotify or YouTube feel in daily life.

### Brand Personality
**Art Yearns Action** — three words: *earthy, purposeful, alive*. The brand is not hype-driven. It has the gravitas of a cultural institution combined with the warmth of a creative community. Think: a Kazakh film retrospective curated with care, not a startup's growth-hacked feed.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Dark-first** (olive-black, not blue-black) — earthy, warm, organic. The palette is sage green + warm cream on deep olive surfaces.
- **Spotify-structure** (sidebar nav, card rows, pill buttons) but with a distinctly Central Asian warmth — not cold Scandinavian minimal.
- **No gradient text**, no glowing accents, no glassmorphism. Clean, intentional.
- Reference: Spotify + MUBI + a Kazakh art catalogue.

### Design Principles
1. **Earthy warmth over tech cool** — olive surfaces, sage accents, cream text. Never pure black or cold gray.
2. **Content-forward** — cards and player should feel like the hero; chrome is quiet.
3. **Purposeful motion** — card hover lift, play button reveal, page transitions. No decoration.
4. **Accessible and multilingual** — KZ/RU/EN, readable at 13–14px body, contrast-safe.
5. **Spotify familiarity, Kazakh soul** — borrowed structural patterns, original visual identity.
